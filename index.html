About Me

Currently a student at Brighton University, studying Digital Media (BA Hons). I love studying at Brighton University because of the large amounts of influential digital companies that are based in the area.

Also got to love the beach, shopping and the doughnuts. Since starting the course my skills in website development have grown vastly.

I am currently half way through my sandwich year, where I was luckily enough to secure myself a placement with MICROS Ecommerce. Here I am part of the studio team as a front-end developer. Here I have been able to use my skill and also gain a load more in wide arrange of softwares and libraries. From being here at my placement it has made me more determined to succeed in building websites.
